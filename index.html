Come visit The Woodbury CT Antiques and Flea Market


We are open Saturday and Sunday mornings from 7:30 am until 2:30 pm. Free admission and free parking!

To become a vendor Contact us or just drive in after 6:30 AM any Saturday or Sunday and you will be given a space (23x20) to set up your table. The vendor fee for Saturday is $20 and the fee for Sunday is $10. Customers are always free! Reduced rates apply to vendors who need more than one space.

For more information please call: (203) 263-6217, email: thenewwoodburyfleamarket@gmail.com , or like us on Facebook!
