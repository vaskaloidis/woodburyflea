  • About us

The Woodbury Antiques and Flea Market has grown to become one of the the best kept secrets in CT. We are a smaller flea market in Woodbury, CT. The flea market focusses on it's role as an antiques market first, utilizing our location in the Woodbury, The Antique Capital.
Vendors at The Woodbury Flea Market sell every type of goods. From Antiques, to furniture, electronics, clothing and much much more. Stop by one weekend to check us out!

Gus and Ann Kaloidis took over the old Woodbury Antiques and Flea Market in 2008 and have been running it as a family business ever since. We have dealers offering a variety of merchandise for sale with an emphasis on early items and antiques. The market is open every Saturday and Sunday.

Vendors can drive to their assigned spaces and set up their tables at 6:30 am. Reservations are not required, walk-ins are welcome, but you may call us to let us know you are coming at: (203) 263-6217. Ask management for special rates.

Customers are invited after 7:30 A.M. We offer ample parking around the restaurant. Parking attendents are available to assist you. Admission is FREE and handicap parking is also available.

This is a continuation of the old Woodbury Antiques and Flea market from around the corner (Route 63 Middle Quarter Road), that had been in business for over forty years. Don and Diane Heavens owned and operated the market until they sold their property in 2006.

The Market is easily accessible from I-84 and it is one and one- half hours away from NY City. The nearest airports are Oxford and Danbury. The Market is located at the bottom of Sherman Hill Road near the intersection of routes 6 and 64.

Woodbury is known for its old houses, antique shops and restaurants. Visitors come from all over every weekend, especially from New York.

Come and enjoy the day!!
